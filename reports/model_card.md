# Model Card

## Intended Use
Educational and research-grade simulation of Canadian bank contagion risk and RL-based portfolio allocation.

## Data Sources
Public market data, configurable Bank of Canada Valet series, manual CSV templates for housing/ETF/CDS data, and synthetic fallback samples.

## Assumptions
Credit spreads, mortgage stress, and exposure profiles may be proxied when real data is unavailable. Graph edges approximate statistical and exposure-based transmission channels.

## Limitations
Not a production risk model. Public data is incomplete, backtests may overfit, and transaction costs/liquidity assumptions are simplified.

## Ethical Considerations
Avoid presenting outputs as recommendations. Use for education, stress-testing methodology, and portfolio research only.

## Financial Disclaimer
Not investment advice. No guarantee of future performance.
